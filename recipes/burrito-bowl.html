Vegan Burrito Bowls

My attempt at burrito bowls

Excellent source of protein that tastes great

Makes four servings

Ingredients

  • 12oz Impossible meat
  • 2 15oz Canned black beans
  • 2 Diced Green Bell Peppers
  • 1 Diced Onion
  • 2 Servings of my Taco Seasoning
  • Rice
  • 7oz Vegan Cheddar

Instructions

  1. Begin by heating two pans to medium-high heat
  2. Add impossible meat to one pan and peppers/onions to the other
  3. Break up impossible and continue stirring both pans until desired doneness
  4. Add a serving of taco seasoning to each pan
  5. Add 2/3 cup water to impossible meat
  6. Add black beans to pan of peppers and onion
  7. Simmer both pans until desired level of moisture
  8. Serve a scoop of beans and beef on a bed of white rice
  9. Sprinkle with vegan cheddar cheese and serve
