Emily's Ziti

Emily's Classic Ziti with a Vegan Twist

A classic dish best enjoyed with friends and family

Makes four servings

Ingredients

  • Ziti Noodles 16oz
  • Impossible Sausage 4pk
  • 20oz Marinara
  • 7oz Vegan Mozzarella

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Begin boiling a pot of water for pasta
  3. Begin heating pan for sausage
  4. Remove sausage from casing
  5. Begin crumbling and cooking sausage in pan for 16 minutes until brown
  6. While sausage is cooking, add pasta to boiling water
  7. After sausage is finished cooking, add marinara to pan and simmer
  8. After cooking, remove pasta from water and set aside
  9. Combine pasta and meat sauce
  10. Lightly grease a pan and add 1/2 of pasta with sauce
  11. Sprinkle 1/3 of your vegan cheese across the pasta
  12. Layer remaining pasta and sprinkle with the rest of your cheese
  13. Bake for 20 minutes
  14. After baking for 20 minutes, remove from oven and let cool before serving
