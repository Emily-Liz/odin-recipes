Butter Tofu

Inspired by classic butter chicken

This vegan twist on butter chicken is an excellent recipe to have under your belt

Makes two large servings

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger minced (optional)
  • 1-2 tbsp garam masala
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 (15oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (13.5oz) can coconut milk (can be subbed by adding an additional 1/4 cup of cashews to recipe)
  • 2 blocks of tofu
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a pot or pan, heat oil over medium-low. Add onion, garlic and cashews to the pot. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the onion is softened and golden, about 10 minutes.
  2. Cook tofu in air fryer or oven, 400 degrees for 20 minutes
  3. Increase the heat to medium. Add spices and ginger and stir until toasted, about 1 minute. Add the crushed tomatoes and gently scrape the pan with spoon to release anything stuck to the bottom.
  4. Transfer contents of pot to blender and blend until smooth. Return to pot.
  5. Add in the coconut milk and tofu to the pot. Stir and reduce heat. Let simmer until the sauce is thickened, about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add butter, sugar and season with salt and pepper to taste, adjust other seasonings as necessary.
